QwertyCraft A Small and Private Minecraft Server

QwertyCraft is a small, relaxed, private vanilla Minecraft server. Anyone is invited to join as long as you follow the two simple rules of no griefing and no stealing. QwertyCraft is maintained, backed up and supervised remotely and regularly.

Server is currently open to ALL users.

Please report any problems to @qwertycraft on Twitter.
